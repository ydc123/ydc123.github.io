Biography

I received my Ph.D. from Tsinghua University in 2024, where I was fortunate to be advised by Prof. Wenjian Yu. During my Ph.D., I conducted research on adversarial attack/defense, AI4EDA, and deep neural network compression. Prior to this, I obtained my bachelor's degree from Tsinghua University in June 2019. I was an intern in the RealAI from November 2018 to December 2024.

Now, I am an algorithm engineer at WeRide, focus on autonomous driving technologies.
